We Are hiring!
2016 02 17



Sapphire is hiring social media editor now
The Sapphire team is dedicated to furthering the exposure of local fashion designers and fashion start-ups. We believe in the power of community, and strive to serve our community through our creativity and delivering advanced and immersive experience. As a professional fashion show agency, we provide new meaningful innovations.

What can you gain in Sapphire –

The Sapphire is designed as a learning environment that allows talented people to have fun while collaborating. With a flat organizational structure, we encourage people to work across teams to create free-flow ideas. Our culture focuses on unleashing the best from individuals. We encourage you passions for fashion.

Job Description –

Sapphire is a new fashion show event design agency. We are looking to expand our voice on social media. We'd like you to post our projects on Twitter, Facebook, and other media to build out social media audience.

Requirements –

- 1-2 years working as a social media editor
- Experience using Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Snapchat, Tumblr and Pinterest
- Strong writing and editing skills
- Confident working independently and productively as a member of a collaborative team
- Full of energy and enthusiasm and eager to work in a fast-paced environment
- Passionate about fashion, art, youth culture, music and design
- Willingness to work weekends during peak times such as fashion weeks

How to apply –
Please submit your resume to szhu@cca.edu along with your blog, youtube channel and Facebook post.
